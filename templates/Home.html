Swift Math

Swift Math, developed by Divyam Banga, Saumya Nagda, Gurjas Dhillon, and Rasesh Patel, is the embodiment of our vision to coach children and adults in learning and enhancing their mental math skills completely free of charge. We have integrated a leaderboard based on the speed and accuracy of the participant when answering questions. This will help create a very competitive landscape for this astonishingly miraculous game. This leaderboard will include participants from all over the world and motivate the competitors to keep playing and beat their personal best records. As a result, people will dramatically improve their mental math skills, completing our goal and vision for this game.
